About me

Teryn is an experienced physiotherapist with 2010 years of practice in complex muscle and joint conditions, concussion rehabilitation, and vestibular treatments.

In addition to her clinical work, Teryn works as a clinical educator serving as an Adjunct Professor with the Physical Therapy department at UBC, and also within the PhysioRefresh program.

Teryn is a Fellow of the Canadian Academy of Manipulative Physiotherapy (FCAMPT), and holds a Masters of Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy and a Masters in Physical Therapy. She has also pursued further qualifications with the Integrated Systems Model, Integrated Dry Needling (previously known as Dry Needling Plus), and is consistently taking further courses to help grow her knowledge and skills.

Skills

Teryn works with patients on a 1:1 basis, tailoring each appointment to meet their specific goals. Whether you're recovering from injury, managing a complex condition, or optimizing movement, she provides personalized, high-quality care.

Her approach combines evidence-informed methods with a friendly, focused style. She views the body as an integrated system, addressing not just symptoms but the broader movement patterns that might contribute to your condition. Through comprehensive assessment and collaborative treatment planning, Teryn is committed to meeting you exactly where you are in your health journey.

She has extensive experience and education to treat with:

  • Manual therapy
  • Dry needling
  • Exercise therapy
  • Concussion rehabilitation
  • Vestibular treatments
  • Strength & conditioning
  • Taping & bracing

She has the skills and expertise to treat a wide range of conditions, with a particular focus on:

  • Neck pain and whiplash
  • Headaches
  • Concussion
  • Vestibular pathologies
  • Back and spine injuries
  • Shoulder injuries
  • Foot and ankle conditions
  • Hip and knee injuries
  • Elbow and wrist injuries
  • Post-operative rehabilitation
