About me

Experienced FCAMPT physiotherapist with expertise in complex muscle and joint conditions, concussion rehabilitation, and vestibular treatments with 2010 years of experience.

Teryn takes a personalized and evidence-informed approach to patient care, and prioritizes active collaboration with both patients and other healthcare providers. Her treatment methodology combines manual therapy, dry needling, and targeted exercises. In addition to her clinical work, Teryn serves as an Adjunct Professor at UBC and has also worked at the University of Queensland, contributing to the education of future physiotherapists.

Skills

I work with you on a 1:1 basis and tailor each appointment to meet your specific goals. Whether you're recovering from injury, managing a complex condition, or seeking to optimize your movement, I've got the skills to help you move forward.

I use personalized and varied approaches in treatment, always collaborating to find the combination that best suits your needs. I have extensive training and experience with:

  • Manual therapy
  • Dry needling
  • Exercise therapy
  • Concussion rehabilitation
  • Vestibular treatments
  • Strength & conditioning
  • Taping & bracing

I work with all kinds of injuries and conditions. I have the most experience with:

  • Neck pain and whiplash
  • Headaches
  • Concussion
  • Vestibular pathologies
  • Back and spine injuries
  • Shoulder injuries
  • Foot & ankle conditions
  • Hip & knee injuries
  • Post-operative rehabilitation

I welcome patients from all backgrounds and walks of life. My commitment is to provide personalized, high-quality care that meets you exactly where you are in your health journey.
